About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

What began as a small dream to bring people together over great food has grown into one of the city’s favorite dining destinations. At Hotel Sai Namaskaram, we believe every meal should be more than just food — it should be an experience of flavors, warmth, and togetherness.

Our multi-cuisine menu celebrates the best of Indian, Chinese, Continental, and Tandoor delicacies — each dish prepared with authentic ingredients, expert techniques, and a touch of love. Whether you’re here for a family dinner, a team lunch, or a cozy meal with friends, we have something special for everyone.

With a perfect blend of traditional hospitality and a modern dining ambiance, Hotel Sai Namaskaram continues to serve happiness on every plate — one delicious memory at a time.
